Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby:

  • Lyrics or Songbook
  • Microphone
  • Practice Space

Steps to Get Started:

  1. Choose a song you love and want to practice.
  2. Find the lyrics and listen to the song carefully.
  3. Warm up your voice with vocal exercises.
  4. Practice singing along with the song to improve your timing and pitch.
  5. Record yourself to track your progress and make improvements.
